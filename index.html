Welcome to Sladebank Woods

Whether this is your first visit or you come here regularly, we invite you to take a moment to find out more about the woods.

We have created a digital woodland trail for you to discover more about the uses, folklore and ecology of some of our trees and grassland areas.

Walk along the surfaced pathway, where you will find posts with QR codes, to immerse yourself in the cycles and seasons of this land.

Instructions

1/ Point your phone camera at the QR code (or use a QR code scanner app). Click on the link that should pop up, directing you to a webpage.

2/ Explore along the pathway to find the 4 posts with a QR code on.
